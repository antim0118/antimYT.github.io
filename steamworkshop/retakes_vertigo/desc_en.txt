[h1][b]   Welcome to Retakes mode![/b][/h1]
[b]   With this map you can train retakes with bots or your friends.[/b]


[h1]   Map rules:[/h1]
[list]
	[*] Every round, all players get one type of weapon (Pistols/SMGs/Rifles).
	[*] Each player receives a different pack of grenades at the beginning of the round.
	[*] Player with the bomb must plant it at the beginning of the round.
	[*] T's should defend the bomb and kill all CT's to win round.
	[*] CT's should defuse the bomb and/or kill all T's to win round and swap sides.
	[*] Any player can use chat commands to toggle something.
[/list]
